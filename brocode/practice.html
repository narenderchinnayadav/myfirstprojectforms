Hi Nanna (transl. Hi Dad) is a 2023 Indian Telugu-language romantic drama film directed by Shouryuv in his directorial debut. The film stars Nani and Mrunal Thakur in the lead roles, alongside Kiara Khanna, Priyadarshi Pulikonda, Angad Bedi, Jayaram and Viraj Ashwin in supporting roles.
